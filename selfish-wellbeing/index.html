Selfishness and wellbeing

Being unselfish is a good long-term strategy for your well being. By being unselfish you gain a reputation for being trustworthy.

Having a reputation for being trustworthy is a superpower. It will help you have strong relationships with people. When things go downhill in life, these relationships will help you. When your business has a setback, your employees, suppliers and customers will stick with you. When your health deteriorates, your friends and family will take care of you.

Being selfish will give you a reputation for being untrustworthy. When things go downhill, you will be on your own. You will be living a harder life. As misfortunes in life are inevitable, being unselfish is easily a better strategy than being selfish for your long-term well being.
